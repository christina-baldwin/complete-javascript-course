Pig Game Instructions

Here you will find the list of instructions on how you can play the Pig Game. First you will need to find a friend to play with, then you can go through these instructions together.

  • 1. Choose who starts first. Whover goes first is Player 1, whoever goes second is Player 2
  • 2. Player 1 clicks the "roll dice" button and the number they have rolled shows up in the "current" box.
  • 3. Player 1 chooses whether to "hold" this number, in which case it gets saved in their score and the turn moves to Player 2 or they can keep rolling and the number gets added to their "current" score.
  • 4. If Player 1 rolls a 1 however, any number in their current score is reset to zero and the turn moves to Player 2.
  • 5. When the turn moves on to Player 2, either through clicking "hold" or through rolling a 1, the same rules apply.
  • 6. The game continues until one of the players reaches a held score of 100. The player to reach this score first, wins! The game can also be reset using "new game".
  • Good luck!
